# Follei – Autonomous Business Workforce Platform

## Project Overview

Follei is an AI-powered Autonomous Business Workforce Platform designed to function as a digital workforce for organizations. Instead of simply automating repetitive tasks, the platform understands business operations, learns organizational knowledge, and autonomously performs customer-facing and revenue-generating activities.

The system integrates with existing business applications, analyzes organizational data, and deploys specialized AI workers capable of handling sales, customer support, collections, customer success, renewals, and operational workflows.

Unlike traditional CRM systems, workflow automation tools, or AI chatbots that execute predefined tasks, Follei makes intelligent decisions based on business context, customer interactions, and continuous learning.

---

# Objective

The primary objective of Follei is to create a unified AI workforce capable of learning an organization's products, services, business processes, policies, customer information, and operational knowledge, enabling AI agents to perform business activities independently while continuously improving through experience.

---

# Problem Statement

Modern organizations rely on multiple disconnected business systems such as:

* Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
* Email Platforms
* WhatsApp Business
* Telephone Systems
* Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
* Learning Management Systems (LMS)
* Ticketing Platforms
* Knowledge Bases
* Spreadsheets
* Internal Documentation

Employees spend considerable time performing repetitive tasks including:

* Lead follow-ups
* Answering customer queries
* Scheduling meetings
* Sending reminders
* Customer onboarding
* Payment collection
* Support ticket handling

Existing AI solutions generally solve only individual problems such as chatbots, AI sales assistants, or workflow automation. However, there is no unified platform capable of acting as an autonomous business workforce.

Follei addresses this challenge by combining business intelligence, knowledge management, customer intelligence, revenue prediction, AI agents, and continuous learning into one integrated platform.

---

# Product Vision

The platform follows a simple vision:

1. Connect business data.
2. Learn business knowledge.
3. Deploy AI workers.
4. Continuously improve through customer interactions.

The goal is to enable organizations to connect their business assets once and immediately deploy AI employees capable of working continuously without manual intervention.

---

# Core Platform Philosophy

Traditional Business Workflow:

Data → Human Decision → Action

Follei Workflow:

Data → Understanding → Decision → Action → Learning

Instead of automating predefined workflows, Follei understands business context, makes intelligent decisions, performs actions, and improves itself after every interaction.

# System 2: Knowledge System

## Purpose

The Knowledge System provides accurate business context to every AI worker.

It consists of four knowledge layers.

### Layer 1 – Structured Knowledge Store

Stores structured business information such as:

* Products
* Services
* Pricing
* Policies
* Plans
* Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

### Layer 2 – Vector Database

Stores unstructured documents including:

* PDFs
* Emails
* Knowledge Articles
* Call Transcripts
* Internal Documents

This layer enables semantic search using vector embeddings.

### Layer 3 – Business Knowledge Graph

Represents relationships between business entities.

Example relationships include:

* Product → Feature
* Feature → Benefit
* Benefit → Customer Segment
* Customer Segment → Objections
* Objection → Response

### Layer 4 – Memory System

Maintains different levels of memory:

* Long-Term Memory (Company Knowledge)
* Mid-Term Memory (Customer History)
* Short-Term Memory (Current Conversation Context)

# System 3: Revenue Intelligence System

## Purpose

The Revenue Intelligence System predicts customer purchase probability and prioritizes leads.

### Lead Intelligence Engine

Calculates multiple business scores including:

* ICP Score
* Intent Score
* Engagement Score
* Qualification Score
* Buying Signal Score
* Relationship Score

### Qualification Framework Engine

Supports industry-standard qualification frameworks including:

* BANT
* MEDDIC
* SPIN
* CHAMP
* ANUM

Organizations can also create custom qualification frameworks.

### Revenue Probability Engine

Predicts the likelihood of customer conversion using:

* Historical Sales Data
* Behavioral Analytics
* Intent Signals
* Qualification Information

The system generates a Revenue Probability Score ranging from 0 to 100.
